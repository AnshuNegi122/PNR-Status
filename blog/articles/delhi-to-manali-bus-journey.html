Delhi to Manali Bus Journey

By Wanderlust Wheels Staff August 23, 2025

A Himalayan Adventure by Bus

Traveling by bus from Delhi to Manali is more than just a journey—it’s an experience! Watch the city fade into rolling hills, pass through pine forests, and catch glimpses of the Beas River winding through the valley. Whether you’re a solo traveler, a group of friends, or a family, the bus ride is a chance to relax, make new friends, and soak in the changing landscapes.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: How long does it take to travel from Delhi to Manali by bus?
A: The journey covers about 540 km and usually takes 12–14 hours, depending on traffic and weather. Overnight buses are a popular choice.

Q: What are the main departure times from Delhi?
A: Buses depart throughout the day, with most services starting in the late afternoon and evening to reach Manali in the morning.

Your Manali Checklist

Manali is a haven for adventure seekers and nature lovers alike. Here are some must-visit places:

  • Hadimba Temple: A peaceful, ancient temple surrounded by a dense cedar forest.
  • Solang Valley: The adventure hub for skiing, paragliding, zorbing, and breathtaking cable car rides.
  • Old Manali: Known for its chill cafes, live music, and bohemian vibe, perfect for unwinding.
  • Rohtang Pass: (Subject to seasonal opening) A high mountain pass offering stunning views and snow activities.
