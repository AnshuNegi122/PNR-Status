Delhi to Bangalore Bus Journey

By Wanderlust Wheels Staff August 23, 2025

The Great Southern Odyssey

A bus journey from Delhi to Bangalore is not for the faint of heart, but it's an epic adventure that crosses the diverse heartland of India. This marathon trip, spanning over 2,000 kilometers, offers a unique perspective on the country's changing landscapes, from the northern plains to the Deccan Plateau. It's a journey for the true road-tripper, offering a tapestry of cultures, cuisines, and geographies.

Preparing for the Long Haul

Q: How long is the bus journey from Delhi to Bangalore?
A: This is one of the longest bus routes in India, typically taking anywhere from 36 to 48 hours, including layovers and rest stops. It's essential to book a comfortable sleeper or semi-sleeper bus for this trip.

Q: What should I pack?
A: Pack light but smart. Essentials include a neck pillow, a light blanket, headphones, a power bank, snacks, and a water bottle. Wet wipes and hand sanitizer are also highly recommended.

Welcome to the Garden City

Upon arriving in Bangalore, the "Silicon Valley of India," you'll be greeted by pleasant weather and a vibrant, cosmopolitan atmosphere. The city is a blend of modern tech parks and lush green spaces.

  • Lalbagh Botanical Garden: Home to a stunning variety of flora and a famous glass house.
  • Bangalore Palace: Explore the opulent Tudor-style architecture and learn about the city's royal history.
  • Commercial Street: A bustling hub for shopping, from street-side stalls to branded showrooms.
  • Innovative Film City: A fun-filled destination with theme parks, museums, and entertainment options.
